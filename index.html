The problem

You're a great coach.
Your backend tells a different story.

Most fitness coaches are managing clients across 4–6 disconnected tools with no unified view of what's happening. The result is always the same.

01
Check-ins fall through the cracks
Client submissions arrive across WhatsApp, email, and DMs. There's no system capturing them — so responses are late, inconsistent, or missed entirely.
02
No clear view of daily priorities
You start each day without knowing which clients need attention, who is at risk, and what is outstanding. You have to reconstruct this from memory, every time.
03
Progress is impossible to track
Client results live in scattered screenshots, WhatsApp chats, and partially filled spreadsheets. There's no clean record of what has changed or what the trend is.
04
Onboarding is reinvented every time
Each new client gets a slightly different experience because there's no documented, repeatable process. Time is lost. Details are missed. Impressions suffer.
05
The backend consumes coaching time
Hours spent switching tools, chasing documents, and trying to remember what each client is doing. Time that should go toward delivering results is going toward managing chaos.
06
It gets worse as you grow
Every new client adds more load to a system that wasn't designed to scale. What's manageable at 5 clients becomes unworkable at 15. The problem compounds.

The process

From first message
to working system.

Every installation follows the same four-phase process. You know exactly what is happening and when.

Qualification

We assess your operation before anything else. You complete two short questionnaires covering your current workflow and business context. We analyse the responses and determine your install tier. This step protects both parties — you only proceed if there's a genuine fit.

2

We issue a written proposal defining exactly what will be installed, at which tier, and on what timeline. Once agreed and signed, we schedule the build. The scope is fixed — no surprises mid-engagement.

The 70/30 model

Not a template.
Not built from scratch.

70% is fixed. Every Coaching OS shares the same structural backbone — the database architecture, relational logic, SOP framework, and dashboard design. This is non-negotiable because it is what makes the system reliable and scalable. It is not a starting point. It is the foundation.

30% is yours. The customisation layer maps the system to how your business actually runs — your programme names, your check-in format, your client terminology, your onboarding specifics. Applied before the build begins. Not bolted on afterward.

The result is a system that works on day one, built around your operation, that does not need to be reassembled when you scale.
